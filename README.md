# Make My Dog Talk

Bring your furry friend to life! "Make My Dog Talk" is a web application that transforms a photo of your dog into a fun, shareable talking video. Simply upload a photo, type a message, and our AI-powered video generation service will create a video of your dog saying your words.

## ‚ú® Features

*   **AI-Powered Video Generation:** Upload a photo of your dog and a script, and our application will generate a video with synchronized lip movements.
*   **Custom Voice Styles:** Specify a voice style (e.g., "deep male voice," "excited puppy") to match your dog's personality.
*   **Secure Payments:** Purchase video credits easily and securely with our Stripe integration.
*   **User Accounts:** Sign in with Google to track your video credits and history.
*   **Admin Bypass:** A special mode for the site owner to bypass credit and rate limits for testing and demos.

"Make My Dog Talk" operates on a credit-based system:

*   **Free Videos:** New users can generate a limited number of videos for free. A cooldown period applies between free generations.
*   **Credit Packs:** To generate more videos or to skip the cooldown, users can purchase credit packs:
    *   **Jump The Line:** 1 credit for a single video.
    *   **3 Video Pack:** 3 credits at a discounted price.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Configuration

Your `.env` file should contain the following variables:

```
# Google Cloud / Vertex AI
GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS=path/to/your/credentials.json
VERTEX_AI_PROJECT_ID=your-gcp-project-id

# Stripe API Keys
STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=pk_...
STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=sk_...
STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=whsec_...

# Stripe Price IDs
VITE_STRIPE_PRICE_ID_3=price_...
VITE_STRIPE_PRICE_ID_10=price_...
VITE_STRIPE_PRICE_ID_25=price_...
STRIPE_PRICE_ID_3=price_...
STRIPE_PRICE_ID_10=price_...
STRIPE_PRICE_ID_25=price_...

# Google OAuth 2.0
GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=your-client-id.apps.googleusercontent.com
GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET=your-client-secret
GOOGLE_REDIRECT_URI=http://localhost:3000/auth/callback

# Admin Bypass
ADMIN_EMAIL=your-admin-email@example.com
```

**Note:** The variables prefixed with `VITE_` are exposed to the frontend.
